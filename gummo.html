— four cameras —

The movie Gummo gives a dystopian feeling of a small town in Ohio, America. Looking at the film we felt that the director emphasize this feeling by using different cameras and in the way he handles them. Four different cameras. To capture these differences, we made a publication based on the four cameras that the director used. The publication is a timeline of the movie from start till the end. Whenever a specific camera is being used in the movie we gave the typography the effect and feeling of it. To strengthen this feeling we chose different types of paper that fit the typography and the camera. This project is made in collaboration with Marc van den Berg.
