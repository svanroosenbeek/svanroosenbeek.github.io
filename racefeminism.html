— race of feminism —

For Race of Feminism we designed a speculative solar-powered motor race in which Saudi Arabia is represented by an all-female team of drivers in the year 2030. The identity of the 2030 event includes fictional sponsorship logos from women’s right groups throughout history, as well as new racing flags, promotion video, fashionable suits and the prototype of a desert-proof racing car all combined into one installation that was shown during Amnesty’s 50th anniversity in Amsterdam. The ultimate goal of the project is to promote gender equality and provide an alternative plus positive future image of women in Saudi Arabia. The installation consists of a suit, video, 3D printed car and posters. In collaboration with Pien Kars and Esther Vane.
