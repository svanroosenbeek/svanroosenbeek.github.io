— about rhythm of the ride and flood —

Michael Joseph Jackson was an American singer, songwriter, record producer and dancer. Called the King of Pop, his contributions to music, dance and fashion along with his publicized personal life made him a global figure in populair culture for over four decades. With this publication I want to show his way of writing music. Each song he wrote started from his imagination. The imagination becomes inspiration, then vocal melodies then to the complete music that we all know. The song that I based the typography on is Billie Jean.
